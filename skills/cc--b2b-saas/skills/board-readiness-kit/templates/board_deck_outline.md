# Board Deck Outline

## Slide 1: Executive Summary
- **Headline:** The one thing you want them to remember.
- **Key Wins:** 3 bullet points.
- **Key Risks:** 1-2 major blockers.
- **The Ask:** What do you need from the board today?

## Slide 2: High-Level Metrics (Flash Report)
- **ARR:** Current vs Plan
- **Burn Rate:** Current vs Plan
- **Runway:** Months remaining
- **NPS/CSAT:** Customer sentiment

## Slide 3: GTM Performance
- **Sales:** Pipeline coverage, Win rates, New logos.
- **Marketing:** Lead volume, CAC, Channel performance.
- **CS:** Net Revenue Retention (NRR), Churn analysis.

## Slide 4: Product Roadmap
- **Shipped:** Key features released last quarter.
- **Upcoming:** Major milestones for next quarter.
- **Adoption:** Usage stats for key features.

## Slide 5: Team & Org
- **Hiring:** Key roles filled vs open.
- **Culture:** eNPS, retention, diversity.

## Slide 6: Financials
- **P&L:** Revenue, COGS, OpEx, EBITDA.
- **Forecast:** Updated projections for EOY.

## Slide 7: Strategic Topic (Deep Dive)
- **Focus:** One major strategic decision or pivot.
- **Options:** A vs B analysis.
- **Recommendation:** What management proposes.

## Slide 8: Appendix
- Detailed cohorts.
- Full pipeline view.
- Org chart.
